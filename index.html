About

Rachel Suffian is a production sound mixer based in Brooklyn, NY with experience in documentary, narrative film, branded content, and cooking videos. Rachel is proud to have recorded production sound on films that have premiered at Sundance and SXSW. Her documentary work has taken her to Italy, India, and all around the East Coast. She is travel ready and looking forward to future projects to take her on adventures.

Rachel is always listening for ways to elevate a story through its sound.
For questions about gear rental or other services, please contact sound@rachelsuffian.com.
