About

Rachel Suffian is a production sound mixer based in Brooklyn, NY who specializes in narrative filmmaking projects including feature films, web series, documentaries, and the occasional fitness video.

She recorded production sound for the short film ‘Wiggle Room’ that played at Sundance 2021 and SXSW 2021.

Rachel is always listening for ways to elevate a story through its sound.
