About

Rachel Suffian is a production sound mixer based in Brooklyn, NY who specializes in narrative filmmaking projects including short films, web series, documentaries, and the occasional fitness video. She also produces the series Drawing with Rachel that is currently making its way through the 2022 festival circuit.

Rachel is always listening for ways to elevate a story through its sound. For questions about gear rental or other services, please contact sound@rachelsuffian.com.
